## 🧠 Semantic Caching System — Goals & Evaluation Metrics

The objective of this project is to design and integrate a **semantic-level caching system** into a multi-stage RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline. Unlike conventional lexical caches, this system operates at the **embedding and semantic similarity level**, reusing intermediate results when queries share meaning, not just words.
This approach aims to improve system speed, reduce compute costs, and maintain semantic consistency across sessions.
